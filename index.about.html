Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning was established at T.John Institute of Technology in the year 2022. The courses are offered under the auspices of VTU and are recognized by Govt. of Karnataka and AICTE, New Delhi. The department seeks to excel as a technology hub processing research and educating students in current technological developments. It is equipped with the state of the art infrastructure and supported by a team of dedicated and qualified staff members. The fast changing scenario in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning necessitates the department to actively extend its focus and activities in research and development, consultancy programs and Industry-Institute Interaction. Being a frontrunner in the field of technology, the department envisages to evolve as a center of excellence, at par with any world class technical institute and to adapt itself to the rapid advancements in various specialized fields.

AI MINDS

AI Minds is a dynamic and innovative club under the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. It serves as a hub for students passionate about AI, machine learning, and data science, providing a collaborative space to learn, research, and develop cutting-edge technologies. Through workshops, hackathons, and expert sessions, AI Minds encourages members to explore real-world applications of AI, enhance their problem-solving skills, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of artificial intelligence. The club fosters a culture of creativity, teamwork, and technical excellence, preparing students for careers in AI-driven industries.

VISION

To enrich students with Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning skills, fostering a culture of research, innovation, and development, thereby empowering them to efficiently address dynamic and evolving societal challenges.

MISSION

  • M1. To provide comprehensive education in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, equipping students with the skills and knowledge to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to the field.
  • M2. To foster a culture of research and innovation by encouraging students to engage in cutting-edge projects, publish their findings, and develop novel AI and ML solutions to address complex real-world problems.
  • M3. To establish strong industry partnerships, enhancing students' practical experience and prepare them for the tech world.
  • M4. To empower students to apply their AI and ML expertise to drive societal progress and address dynamic challenges in various sectors.
