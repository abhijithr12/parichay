PARICHAY 3.0

What is PARICHAY?

Parichay is a techfest organized by the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. The event aims to ignite student's passion for technology by providing a platform to explore, innovate, and compete in various technical domains. Through engaging competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions, Parichay fosters a culture of learning, creativity, and problem-solving, encouraging students to enhance their technical knowledge and skills.

EVENTS

IDEATHON

Participants will collaborate in teams to identify real-world tech challenges, propose innovative solutions, and present their ideas, competing for the Ideathon title.

TREASURE HUNT

Participants will be given technical questions which they have to solve and reach the ending point.

MOCK INTERVIEW

Participants will divided as intervie and interviewer.

BYTE BOUNTY (Battle of Innovation)⚔

Participants will compete in teams of 2-4 through three rounds—problem statement identification, catapult engineering, and a tech debate—testing their innovation, technical skills, and argumentation, with the highest-scoring team winning the BYTE BOUNTY (Battle of Innovation)⚔️ title.

GAME DEBUGGING

Participants will be given a game which they have to debug the error and then win it.

Prizes & Certificates

Winners will receive exciting rewards and recognition for their innovative solutions.
